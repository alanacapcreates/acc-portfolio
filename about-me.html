<About Me/>

I am a recent graduate of St. Joseph's College, with a B.S. in Computer Science and a minor in Digital Design. I am an aspiring UX Developer hoping to use my creativity and problem solving skills within a UX design team.

I have experience with the Adobe Creative Suite, HTML, CSS, Javascript, and more! I am eager to learn, and can adapt to new environments. ‍

My Computer Science education has taught me the technicalities behind a design. Having this knowledge helps me accurately map out design elements. By combining these methodologies with my digital design background, I can further understand the “how” + “why” within a successful user experience.

I am always looking for new projects to tackle, so feel free to contact me. ‍
